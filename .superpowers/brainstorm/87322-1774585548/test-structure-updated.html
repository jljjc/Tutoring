Writing Component — How it works in the PoC
Delivery: Student is given a prompt (e.g. "Write a story beginning with…") and types their response within the time limit.
Scoring: Claude scores on 5 criteria — Ideas & Content, Structure & Organisation, Vocabulary, Grammar & Punctuation, Spelling.
Tutoring: Personalised written feedback on what specifically to improve, with a follow-up prompt to practise the weakest criterion.
